Our Mission

Our mission is to provide satisfaction guaranteed acclaimed and structured courses in Coding, Robotics, Math, ELA and public speaking to prepare your child for the digital and competitive age!!
Certified Instructors

Our certified/professional teachers bring in their well-versed knowledge in structured and practical method during their classes which reflects on the individual student learning needs.

Student Teacher Ratio

Our student to teacher ratio is 6:1 for most of our group programs. This will allow personalized focus on each kid and allow better interaction with you child so they can learn to their highest capability.

Progress Report

Our instructors take personal time and provide the weekly/overall progress of the student for their respective course.

Project Based Learning

Our project-based learning helps students to explore and find solutions for real world problems/challenges in different areas of coding, public speaking, etc. This helps students acquire widened/deeper knowledge and real-time problem-solving approach.

Common Core Curriculum

Our Math and ELA programs are based on the common core curriculum for each grade level which are high quality standards and enables the student to excel at the end of each grade.

Assisted Learning

Students have opportunity to review the videos, course material, reach out to instructors outside of the regular course timings so their curiosity for learning can be achieved anytime with right assistance.
